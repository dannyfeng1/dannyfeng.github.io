About Me

I've always had a passion for figuring things out. During my undergraduate at UCSD, I utilized biochemical experiments to extrapolate data from tissue samples in order to identify the root of genetic diseases and any possible treatments for them. Nowadays, I utilize computer programs to solve my problems. There's still human error but at least debugging doesn't require setting up another week long experiment.

In my free time, I continue to problem solve by optimizing video game strategies, trying out new bouldering/rock climbing routes, and piecing together the clues in a Dungeons and Dragons campaign!
